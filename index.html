1. Friday the 13th

Which famous horror movie franchise centres around a killer named Jason Voorhees?

  • A. Halloween
  • B. Friday the 13th
  • C. A Nightmare on Elm Street
  • D. Saw
Correct Answer: B
Verification: Jason Voorhees is the central antagonist of the Friday the 13th film franchise, which began in 1980.

2. Australian Coat of Arms

Which native animals appear on Australia’s coat of arms?

  • A. Kangaroo and Emu
  • B. Koala and Wombat
  • C. Kangaroo and Platypus
  • D. Emu and Cassowary
Correct Answer: A
Verification: The Australian coat of arms features a red kangaroo and an emu, two widely recognised native animals.

4. Which Is NOT a Country

Which of these is not a real sovereign country?

  • A. Bhutan
  • B. Estonia
  • C. Tuvalu
  • D. Kharistan
Correct Answer: D
Verification: Bhutan, Estonia, and Tuvalu are internationally recognised sovereign states. Kharistan is fictional.

7. Hydrofoil

What is a hydrofoil?

  • A. A submarine navigation device
  • B. A vessel whose hull is lifted above the water by underwater hydrofoil wings
  • C. A reinforced ship hull foil

Correct Answer: B
Verification: Hydrofoils use underwater wing structures that lift the hull above water, reducing drag and increasing speed.

4. National Symbols

Which animals appear on Australia's coat of arms?

  • A. Kangaroo and Emu
  • B. Koala and Wombat
  • C. Kangaroo and Platypus

Science

5. Physics

What is a hydrofoil?

  • A. A submarine navigation device
  • B. A boat lifted above the water by underwater wings that generate lift
  • C. A reinforced ship hull plate
  • D. A waterproof aircraft wing
Correct Answer: B
Verification: Hydrofoils use submerged wings to lift the hull above the water, reducing drag and increasing speed.
